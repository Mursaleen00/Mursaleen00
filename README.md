> *Turning EU flight disruption law into a 3-minute digital claim process.*

[![Flight Wiser Claims](https://portfolio-backend-ruddy-chi.vercel.app/projects/flight-wiser-claims.png)](https://flightwiserclaims.com/)

| | |
|:---|:---|
| **Category** | Full Stack |
| **Tech** | Next.js 15 · TypeScript · Tailwind CSS 4 · Cloudinary |

**💡 Problem:** Air passengers across the EU and UK regularly lose up to €600 in rightful compensation for delayed or cancelled flights — simply because the claims process is complex, inaccessible, and manually overwhelming.

**🚀 Solution:** Built a professional, multi-step flight compensation platform that guides passengers through the entire claims process. Clean intake forms, structured data capture, and a production-grade Next.js 15 architecture handle claims at scale with zero friction.

**📈 Result:** Reduced claim submission time from hours to under 3 minutes. Enabled the platform to scale claim volumes without adding support overhead — a direct impact on business revenue and user retention.
